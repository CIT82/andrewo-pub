Hydration

Hydration is essential for maintaining optimal health and well-being. Water makes up a significant portion of our body, and staying hydrated helps regulate body temperature, support digestion, and enhance cognitive function. Proper hydration also aids in nutrient absorption, muscle recovery, and the removal of toxins from the body. Whether you're exercising, working, or relaxing, drinking enough water throughout the day is key to feeling energized, focused, and balanced. Remember, hydration isn’t just about drinking water—it's about nourishing your body with what it needs to function at its best!

Water

Daily
Men: ~8 water bottles
Women: ~6 water bottles
  • Hydrates & prevents dehydration
  • Regulates body temperature
  • Aids digestion & nutrient absorption
  • Flushes toxins & supports detox
  • Lubricates joints for better movement
  • Boosts energy & improves focus
  • Enhances skin health
  • Supports metabolism
  • Controls appetite

Creatine

Daily
3-5 grams (Non-Loading Phase)
  • Increases muscle strength and power
  • Enhances exercise performance, especially in high-intensity activities
  • Supports muscle growth by increasing water retention in muscles
  • Improves recovery after intense workouts
  • Boosts brain function and may help with cognitive tasks
  • Supports energy production during short bursts of activity
  • Helps prevent muscle damage and reduce inflammation
  • May improve endurance over time with regular use
