Yoga

Yoga is for strengthening the body, calming the mind, and improving overall well-being. It enhances flexibility, balance, and endurance while also reducing stress and anxiety through mindful breathing and movement. Yoga can be used for relaxation, physical fitness, mental clarity, and spiritual growth, making it a versatile practice for people of all lifestyles and fitness levels. Whether you want to build strength, relieve tension, or find inner peace, yoga provides a path to a healthier and more balanced life.

Hatha

Foundation of Balance and Breath
30-60 min

Hatha Yoga is perfect for building a strong foundation in yoga. With a focus on controlled movements, deep breathing, and alignment, this practice enhances flexibility, balance, and relaxation. Ideal for beginners and those seeking a mindful, steady approach to wellness.

Bikram

Sweat, Detox, and Strength
90 min

Bikram Yoga is an intense 90-minute practice done in a heated room, following a set sequence of 26 postures. The heat promotes flexibility, detoxification, and endurance, making it a powerful practice for those looking to challenge both body and mind.

Power

Intensity, Strength, and Flow
45-75 min

Power Yoga is a fast-paced, dynamic workout designed to build strength, endurance, and flexibility. With continuous movement and challenging poses, this high-energy practice is perfect for those looking to break a sweat while improving both physical and mental resilience.
